Chrome Extension · No backend · Free
Step 1 of 4

Install the Extension

Load the extension into Chrome in developer mode — no Chrome Web Store needed.

  • 1
    Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  • 2
    Toggle Developer mode on (top-right corner)
  • 3
    Click Load unpacked and select the NotifyMe folder
  • 4
    Click the 🧩 puzzle icon in your toolbar and pin NotifyMe
Step 2 of 4
